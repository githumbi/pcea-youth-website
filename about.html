About P.C.E.A Gakoe youth

Our youth is part of PCEA Gakoe church located at Ngewa Division. It is one of the four Kambui Parish Churches which are Kambui, Mitahato and Anima The youth in Gakoe Church are approximately 80 and the number keeps growing on. Our Objective is helping in making a better tommorrow for everyone by Spreading the love of God to those who recquire it most. We have done it by visiting orphans homes and the sick also.

Our Leaders

"A flock without a shephard is like a flock following a lions den." We believe that leadership brings order within ourselves. The youth Committee is led by our patron Mr. George Njoroge who has led the youth committe with wisdom and with a main aim of getting the best from them. Weekly meetings

Meetings are held every day,each day with different agendas. members meet for a fellowship which is led by the current chaiman_allan Peter kuria. The main objective for the fellowship is to bring all the youths together and more so have a worship experience. All the meetings start from 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm.

Monday

Members meet for a fellowship which is led by the current chaiman_Allan Peter kuria. The main objective for the fellowship is to bring all the youths together and more so have a worship experience.

Tuesday

Members meet for a bible study which is led by the Bible study coordinator- Zipporah nyambura.

Wednesday

The dance crew practices the dance moves to be presented to the church congregation on Sunday.

Thursday

Praise in worship team including the instrumentalists learn new songs and also practices a presentation during the main service.

Friday

Prayers are held which are led by the prayer coordinator - Elizabeth wangeci. Members are expected to pray for the prayer items raised.

Saturday

Members meet for games and other fun activities. Generally the day is referred to as funday. The members can also decide to engage with the society for example visiting the less fortunate.

Sunday

Members meet for revivals where youths of other churches are invited. Basically the revivals involves all the youths to a service, where they grow spiritually and also helps them discover their talents.
