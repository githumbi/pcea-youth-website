YOUTH WEEK

The youth week started on 24th April, which was officially opened by the youth chairman sir allan kuria.

On Tuesday 25th April, all the present youth members engaged with the society, where they visited two less fortunate homes.

On Wednesday 26th April, youths showed their various talents. This included dancing, acting, singing and playing instruments. They also rehearsed for presentation to be staged on Sunday.

On Thursday 27th April, the youths planted flowers and trees to mark youth's environment day.

On Friday 28th April, the youths together joined for prayers and a worship experience to ask the almighty for a successful fundraising on Sunday 30th April.

On Saturday the youths engaged in sports and games and preparation were made thorough for the next day ceremony. The week was a success, and we were able to raise enough funds to keep us moving. Thank you to the Almighty, we praise God for this far. AMEN

ABOUT OUR CHURCH
